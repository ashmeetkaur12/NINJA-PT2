NINJA: NON INVASIVE NEONATE JAUNDICE ESTIMATION

NINJA (Non-invasive Neonate Jaundice Assessment) is an AI-powered research initiative aimed at developing a safe, contactless method for estimating bilirubin levels in newborns using skin images. Traditional methods for jaundice detection often require invasive blood tests, which can be stressful for both infants and caregivers. NINJA leverages deep learning techniques to analyze visual cues from skin images and predict Total Serum Bilirubin (TSB) levels accurately. By integrating advanced AI with accessible imaging, the project aspires to support early diagnosis and reduce the dependence on invasive procedures, especially in resource-limited settings.
